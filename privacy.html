Welcome to the Privacy Policy for NH Web Design ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate under the domain nhwebdesign.co.uk and are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected from you strictly for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your direct project inquiries or service messages.

  • To provide you with information regarding our website design, graphic design, branding, or web application services.

4. Cookies and Third-Party Processors

We use third-party services on our website to ensure functionality, security, and performance tracking. These processors may set cookies or handle your data:

  • Formspree: Our contact forms are processed securely through Formspree. Data submitted through forms is transmitted and stored on their servers to allow us to read and respond to your emails.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile: We utilise Cloudflare Turnstile for anti-bot validation and security. Turnstile processes telemetry signals (like browser features) to detect automated spam attempts without tracking personal user habits.
  • Google Analytics (GA4): We use Google Analytics cookies to monitor general website traffic behavior and improve user experience. This data is fully anonymised.

5. Data Retention & Security

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. We implement strict digital safeguards to ensure your form data remains confidential and secure against unauthorised access.

Under the UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, restrict, or request the erasure of the personal data we hold about you. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
